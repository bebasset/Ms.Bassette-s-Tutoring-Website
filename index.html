A note on in-person sessions

For everyone's safety and comfort, in-person tutoring is held only in public locations — such as a local library, bookstore café, or community center. I do not meet students in private homes or other private spaces. Once you book, we'll agree on a public spot that's convenient for you.

About Ms. Felicity

I teach elementary school during the year, and tutor over the summer so students can keep their momentum — or catch up on what didn't quite click during the school year.

Every student learns differently. I'll start by figuring out what your child already knows and where they're getting stuck, then build sessions around that. Reading, writing, math, study habits — whatever they need.

There's no fixed age range. If your student is older or younger than typical elementary, reach out — I'm happy to talk and see if I'm a good fit.

Quick questions

What grades or ages do you tutor?

No fixed age group. I primarily teach elementary, but I'm open to older and younger students — message me and we'll figure out if I'm a good fit.

What subjects do you cover?

Reading, writing, math, study skills, and general homework help. If you have something specific in mind, ask first.

How does payment work?

Payment is collected at the start of each session — cash, Venmo, or Zelle. Details go out with your booking confirmation.

Can we meet at my home?

No — in-person sessions are held in public locations only (libraries, bookstore cafés, community centers). Zoom is a great option if a public meet-up isn't convenient.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

No problem — please give at least 24 hours' notice when possible so the slot can go to someone else.
